## PRO TIPS:
1. **Post at 6-8 PM** — that's when homeowners are scrolling
2. **Always include your phone number** in captions
3. **Reply to EVERY comment and DM** within 1 hour
4. **Use location tags** — tag "Ventura, California" on every post
5. **Share posts to your Story** every time
6. **Save your best content as Highlights** on Instagram (Gates, Railings, Doors, Reviews)
7. **Ask customers to tag you** in their posts after installation
8. **Film 10-second clips** of EVERY job — you'll always have content
