## SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

### Step 1: Create Google Ads Account
1. Go to **ads.google.com**
2. Sign in with legendarymetalworksinc@gmail.com
3. Choose "Switch to Expert Mode" (bottom of the page) — DON'T use Smart Campaign
4. Choose campaign goal: **Leads** > **Phone calls**

### Step 2: Campaign Settings
- Campaign type: **Search**
- Networks: **Google Search only** (uncheck Display Network)
- Location: **Ventura County, CA** + **Calabasas, CA** + **Malibu, CA**
  - Use "People IN or regularly IN these locations"
  - Radius: 35 miles from Ventura, CA
- Language: English
- Budget: **$5/day**
- Bidding: **Maximize clicks** (to start, switch to Maximize conversions after 30 days)

### Step 3: Schedule
- Run ads **Monday - Saturday only** (match your business hours)
- Hours: **7 AM - 8 PM**

## TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION

### Week 1-2: Learning Phase
- Don't change anything. Let Google learn which keywords work.
- Check daily that your budget isn't overspending.

### Week 3-4: Optimize
- Pause keywords with 0 clicks after 100+ impressions
- Increase bids on keywords getting conversions (calls/form fills)
- Add new negative keywords for irrelevant searches (check "Search Terms" report)

### Month 2+:
- Switch bidding to "Maximize Conversions" once you have 15+ conversions
- Create new ad variations to test
- Consider increasing budget to $10/day if ROI is positive
