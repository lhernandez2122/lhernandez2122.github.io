**Ad Set 1 — Ventura County Homeowners**
- Location: **35 mile radius from Ventura, CA**
- Age: **30-65**

- Custom Audience: People who visited legendarymetalworks.com in the last 30 days

## LEAD FORM SETUP (INSTANT FORMS)

Use Meta's **Instant Form** (Lead Form) instead of sending people to your website. This keeps them on Facebook/Instagram and captures their info with 2 taps. MUCH higher conversion rate.

### Form fields:
1. Full Name (auto-filled from Facebook)
2. Phone Number (auto-filled)
3. Email (auto-filled)
4. **Custom question:** "What are you interested in?" (dropdown)
   - Driveway Gate
   - Railings / Balcony
   - Metal Front Door
   - Fencing
   - Side Gate
   - Custom Project
   - Not Sure Yet
