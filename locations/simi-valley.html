Simi Valley's Trusted Metal Fabrication Experts

Simi Valley presents a unique combination of wide suburban lots, hilltop estates, and fire-adjacent open land that makes quality metal fabrication not just a cosmetic choice — it's a practical one. From the manicured golf-course homes of Wood Ranch to the elevated properties overlooking the Reagan Library, Legendary Metal Works Inc. has been building custom metalwork worthy of this community since 2000.

Three generations of family craftsmanship and 500+ completed projects mean we understand the full range of Simi Valley's character: the HOA-governed planned communities of Big Sky and Madera that demand architectural precision, the hillside properties near Simi Hills that benefit from non-combustible metal perimeter fencing as a fire defense layer, and the classic family neighborhoods of West Simi and Strathearn where curb appeal and security go hand in hand. Our Ventura fabrication shop is just 25 minutes away — close enough for fast service, built with the care of a neighborhood craftsman.

Simi Valley's dry-heat climate and position within Southern California's wildfire corridor make metal fencing an especially smart investment. Unlike wood, steel and wrought iron do not ignite, warp, or decay. A well-designed metal fence creates a non-combustible perimeter barrier that holds up through summer fire season and outlasts any wooden alternative by decades — all while looking far more distinguished.

Frequently Asked Questions — Simi Valley

How much does a driveway gate cost in Simi Valley?
Most Simi Valley driveway gate projects range from $8,000 to $35,000 depending on driveway width, material selection (wrought iron, steel, aluminum), gate style, and the automation system. Estate properties in Wood Ranch or Simi Hills with wider openings and custom ornamental designs typically fall toward the higher end. We provide free on-site estimates with a fully detailed written quote — no surprises.
How long does a metal gate or railing project take?
Most Simi Valley residential projects take 6 to 10 weeks from signed agreement to completed installation — covering design, fabrication at our Ventura shop, permit processing, and on-site installation. If your project requires HOA architectural review, budget an additional 1–2 weeks for that process. We provide a clear project timeline at the estimate stage so you always know where things stand.
Are you licensed to work in Simi Valley?
Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. holds California Contractor License #1119503 and is fully licensed, bonded, and insured to perform metalwork and structural installations throughout Ventura and Los Angeles counties, including Simi Valley. You can verify our license status at the California Contractors State License Board (CSLB) website.
Do I need a permit for a gate or fence in Simi Valley?
Yes. The City of Simi Valley requires building permits for motorized driveway gates and fencing that exceeds certain height thresholds. We manage the entire permitting process — applications, plan submissions, and final inspections — on your behalf. Our familiarity with Simi Valley's building department means fewer delays and faster approvals.
Can metal fencing help protect my property from wildfires?
Absolutely. Unlike wood fencing, steel and wrought iron do not ignite, making them the preferred perimeter choice for Simi Valley properties in fire-prone zones, hillside areas, and high fire hazard severity zones (HFHSZ). Metal fencing creates a non-combustible barrier that can slow fire spread and is often viewed favorably by homeowners insurance carriers in wildfire-risk areas. It's one of the most practical upgrades a Simi Valley hillside homeowner can make.
Do you work with HOA guidelines in Wood Ranch and Big Sky?
Yes. We regularly design gates and fencing that comply with HOA CC&Rs in Wood Ranch, Big Sky, Madera, and other Simi Valley planned communities. Before finalizing any design, we review your association's architectural standards and can prepare complete submittal packages — drawings, material specs, and finish samples — for your HOA review board. Our goal is to get your project approved on the first submission.
