Moorpark's Go-To Custom Metal Fabrication Shop

Moorpark has a character all its own. Tucked between rolling hills and open ranchland at the eastern edge of Ventura County, it's one of the last communities in the region where large lots, working ranches, and equestrian properties are still the norm rather than the exception. That rural feel is exactly why property owners here need metalwork built to a different standard — and why Legendary Metal Works has been serving Moorpark for over two decades.

A standard off-the-shelf gate won't cut it on a multi-acre equestrian estate or a long gravel ranch driveway. We fabricate everything custom at our shop in Ventura — just 20 minutes from Moorpark via the 118 freeway. That means heavy-gauge steel driveway gates engineered for long rural entrances, pipe fencing designed to keep horses safe and contained, and ornamental railings for the newer homes in Campus Park and Mountain Meadows. Every project is built from scratch to fit your property, your terrain, and your design vision.

Family-owned since 2000, Legendary Metal Works brings three generations of metalworking knowledge to every project in Moorpark. We've completed 500+ installations across Ventura County, and we handle the entire process — design, permits, fabrication, and installation — under one roof.

Frequently Asked Questions — Moorpark

Can you build gates for ranch properties and equestrian estates in Moorpark?

Absolutely. Ranch and equestrian properties are one of our core specialties. We fabricate heavy-duty driveway gates designed for long ranch entrances — often 16 to 24 feet wide — using thicker gauge steel and commercial-grade motors built for daily use on unpaved or gravel driveways. We also build ranch-style pipe gates and manual swing gates for secondary property entrances and paddock access.

Do you install equestrian and horse property fencing in Moorpark?

Yes. We build pipe fencing, no-climb wire fencing, and custom metal corral systems specifically designed for equestrian properties. Our fencing is built to be safe for horses — no sharp edges, proper height, and durable construction that holds up to years of use. We can match existing fencing or design a completely new layout for your paddocks and pastures.

How much do driveway gates cost in Moorpark?

Most Moorpark driveway gate projects range from $8,000 to $35,000 depending on driveway width, terrain, design, and automation. Ranch properties with long driveways or sloped terrain often require custom engineering that affects the final cost. We provide free on-site estimates so you get an accurate number based on your specific property — no guessing.

How long does a gate project take in Moorpark?

From signed contract to completed installation, most Moorpark gate projects are finished in 6 to 10 weeks. This covers design, permit processing, fabrication at our Ventura shop, and installation at your property. Larger ranch or equestrian fencing projects may take slightly longer depending on scope and site conditions.

Do I need a permit for gates or fencing in Moorpark?

Generally, yes. The City of Moorpark requires permits for motorized driveway gates and fencing above certain heights. Properties in unincorporated Ventura County areas near Moorpark fall under county jurisdiction with separate requirements. We handle the entire permit process — drawings, submittals, and inspections — as part of your project so you don't have to deal with the paperwork.

Is Legendary Metal Works licensed in California?

Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. holds California Contractor License #1119503. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured for all metal fabrication and installation work in Moorpark and throughout Ventura County. You can verify our license at the California Contractors State License Board website.
