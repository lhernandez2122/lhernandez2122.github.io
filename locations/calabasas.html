Calabasas' Premier Custom Metal Fabrication

Calabasas sets the standard for luxury living in Los Angeles County. From the ultra-private enclaves of Hidden Hills to the architecturally distinguished estates along the Mulholland Corridor, this market demands metalwork that matches its prestige — and its unique challenges. Legendary Metal Works Inc. has been delivering that standard since 2000, with three generations of fabricators who understand what luxury homeowners expect and what local fire codes and HOAs require.

Privacy and security define the Calabasas buyer. Our motorized estate gates are engineered for complete visual privacy — solid steel panel or custom lattice designs, dual-swing or cantilever-slide configurations for wide estate driveways — with advanced access control baked in: intercom integration, camera-linked entry, smartphone operation, license plate recognition, and battery backup. Every gate is custom-fabricated in our Ventura shop and finished to complement your home's exterior palette.

The 2018 Woolsey Fire reshaped how Calabasas homeowners think about their properties. Much of the Calabasas foothills and Mulholland corridor sits in a High or Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone. Steel and iron are non-combustible — and the right choice for gates, fencing, and railings where fire resistance matters. We are familiar with the Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) code requirements that apply to many Calabasas properties and design accordingly.

Our fabrication shop is located in Ventura, approximately 30 minutes from Calabasas via the 101 freeway. We handle every phase in-house: design, engineering, fabrication, finishing, and installation — with no subcontracting of the metalwork itself.

Frequently Asked Questions — Calabasas

How much does a custom driveway gate cost in Calabasas?

Custom driveway gates in Calabasas typically range from $8,000 to $35,000 depending on size, design complexity, materials, and automation systems. Hidden Hills estate entries or fully custom privacy gates with advanced security integration may exceed that range. We provide free, itemized on-site estimates so you know exactly what you are investing in before committing to anything.

How long does a custom gate project take?

Most custom gate projects in Calabasas take 6 to 10 weeks from signed contract to installation. That covers design approval, material procurement, fabrication, finishing, and on-site installation. HOA architectural review or permit processing may extend the timeline slightly — we account for that upfront in our scheduling so there are no surprises.

Are you licensed to work in Calabasas and Hidden Hills?

Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. holds California Contractor License #1119503 and is fully insured. We pull all required permits through the City of Calabasas and Los Angeles County and are experienced navigating HOA architectural review in communities like The Oaks of Calabasas and Hidden Hills.

Can you build privacy and security gates for Hidden Hills estates?

Absolutely. Hidden Hills is one of the most privacy-sensitive communities in Los Angeles County, and we design accordingly. Our estate gates feature solid steel panel construction for complete visual privacy, dual-swing or cantilever-slide configurations for wide estate driveways, integrated camera and intercom systems, license plate recognition, and smartphone-controlled access — all within a design that complements your architecture.

Do your products meet fire-zone requirements for Calabasas?

Yes. Much of Calabasas and the surrounding Mulholland corridor sits in a High or Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone, as the 2018 Woolsey Fire made clear. We fabricate gates and fencing in steel and iron — non-combustible materials — and apply coatings that meet CAL FIRE requirements. We are familiar with the Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) code requirements for properties in these zones and can advise on compliant designs from the start.

Do you work with HOA requirements in The Oaks of Calabasas?

Yes. Communities like The Oaks of Calabasas, Calabasas Hills, and Park Moderne have specific HOA guidelines covering gate styles, heights, materials, and finishes. We review your HOA CC&Rs before beginning design, ensure our proposals comply fully, and can handle the architectural review submission and attend approval meetings if needed.

How far is your shop from Calabasas?

Our fabrication shop is at 4882 McGrath St, Unit 110, Ventura, CA 93003 — approximately 30 minutes from Calabasas via the 101 freeway. We serve clients throughout Calabasas, Hidden Hills, and the greater Los Angeles area. Site visits, installations, and service calls are all part of our project process.
