Camarillo's Trusted Metal Fabrication Company

Legendary Metal Works Inc. has been crafting custom metalwork for Ventura County since 2000 — 25+ years, three generations of family craftsmanship, and 500+ completed projects. Our Ventura shop is just 15 minutes down the 101 from Camarillo, which means faster site visits, tighter scheduling, and installations that don't drag on.

Camarillo is one of Ventura County's most distinctive markets. Spanish Hills estates demand ornate ironwork with authentic scroll and hammer detailing. The agricultural properties around Somis need heavy-duty perimeter fencing that's both functional and attractive. Camarillo Heights' elevated lots call for custom railings that frame sweeping views without blocking them. Las Posas Estates and the growing communities along Las Posas Road expect premium finishes that match their investment-grade homes. We understand these nuances — and we build accordingly.

Every project begins with a free on-site consultation. We measure your property, discuss your vision and HOA requirements, handle permit drawings, and fabricate everything right here in Ventura County — no outsourcing, no middlemen.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Camarillo

What does a custom driveway gate cost in Camarillo? +

Most residential driveway gate projects in Camarillo range from $8,000 to $35,000, depending on gate size, style, automation, and finish. A straightforward single swing gate with basic automation sits at the lower end; a dual-swing estate entry with ornate Spanish scrollwork and smart-access automation sits at the higher end. Every project receives a free on-site quote — call (805) 396-9984 to schedule yours.

How long does a custom metal project take in Camarillo? +

Most projects run 6 to 10 weeks from signed contract to completed installation — this includes design finalization, permit processing, fabrication, and installation. Projects with extensive Spanish scroll detailing or that require HOA architectural review may run toward the longer end. Because our shop is only 15 minutes away in Ventura, site visits and deliveries don't add extra delays. We provide a clear project timeline before work begins.

Do you handle HOA approvals for Spanish Hills and other Camarillo communities? +

Yes. HOAs in Spanish Hills, Las Posas Estates, and other Camarillo planned communities typically require architectural committee approval before any gate, fence, or railing project. We prepare the design drawings, material specifications, and finish samples required for submittal. We've worked through the HOA process many times and know how to present projects in a way that gets approved quickly. You stay in the loop without doing the legwork.

What permits are required for gates and fences in Camarillo? +

The City of Camarillo requires building permits for most driveway gates and fences exceeding certain height thresholds. We handle the complete permit process — drawing preparation, application filing, and coordination with Camarillo's building department. As a CA Licensed contractor (#1119503), we're authorized to pull permits on your behalf, which simplifies the process and protects your property's legal standing.

Can you do perimeter or agricultural fencing for Somis properties? +

Absolutely. Agricultural and rural properties in Somis and the Las Posas Valley often need heavy-duty perimeter fencing that's both secure and visually appropriate for the landscape. We fabricate and install ornamental iron and steel fencing suited to large rural parcels — including custom entry gates with remote access, cattle guards, and perimeter fencing designed to define property boundaries while maintaining the open character of the land.

Can you create authentic Spanish-style ironwork for Camarillo homes? +

Spanish-style ironwork is one of our specialties. Camarillo's Spanish Colonial and Mediterranean architecture calls for hand-forged scrolls, twisted pickets, hammered textures, and arched designs. We study the specific style of your home — whether a traditional hacienda in Spanish Hills or a Mission Revival property near Old Town — and craft pieces that look like they belong to the original construction, not like a modern addition.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Camarillo? +

Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. holds California Contractor License #1119503 and carries full liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We're fully authorized to fabricate and install metalwork in Camarillo and throughout Ventura County. Our license information is available for verification through the California Contractors State License Board (CSLB).
