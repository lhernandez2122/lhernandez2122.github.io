FAQ

Ventura Homeowners Ask Us...

Straight answers on prices, permits, and how we work in Ventura.

How much does a custom driveway gate cost in Ventura?

Most of our motorized driveway gates in Ventura fall between $8,000 and $35,000 installed. Price drivers: width, material (wrought iron vs steel vs aluminum), ornamental detail, motor system, and site conditions like trenching for power. Every quote is line-itemed so you see exactly what every dollar pays for. Financing available.

Do I need a permit for a driveway gate in Ventura?

Usually yes. The City of Ventura typically requires a building permit for new driveway gates, and unincorporated Ventura County (Hillside, Montalvo edges) has its own rules. We handle every permit as part of our service — submittals, renderings, and approvals are all included in the quote. You don't touch a single form.

How long does a project take from first call to installation?

Free consultation within 3–5 days • Design and quote within 48 hours • Permits (if required) 2–6 weeks • Shop fabrication 3–6 weeks • Install 1–5 days. Most full projects wrap 6–10 weeks from the day you sign off on the design. Rush schedules available for an additional fee.

Are you licensed and insured in Ventura County?

Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. is a California-licensed contractor (Lic #1119503), fully bonded and insured with general liability and workers' comp. We'll email certificates of insurance before any contract is signed — just ask.

My house is in Pierpont / the Keys / Ventura Harbor — will salt air ruin my gate?

Not if we build it right. Homes within 2 miles of the ocean get our coastal package: 316 marine-grade stainless hardware, hot-dipped galvanizing, zinc-rich primer, marine powder coat, and stainless fasteners throughout. Adds 10–20% to the cost, roughly doubles the lifespan. Cheap imported steel rusts in two winters — ours won't.

Do you restore historic wrought iron on Downtown and Midtown homes?

Yes — one of our specialties. Ventura's older neighborhoods have beautiful original wrought iron that's worth saving. We repair rusted sections, re-weld failing joints, restore ornamental scrollwork, and refinish with period-appropriate coatings. We can also fabricate matching pieces if a section is beyond repair.
