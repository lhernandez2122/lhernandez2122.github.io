Who We Are

Where Craftsmanship Meets Innovation

Legendary Metal Works Inc. was founded on a simple belief: metalwork should be more than functional — it should be extraordinary. What began as a small workshop over 25 years ago has grown into one of Ventura County's most trusted names in custom metal fabrication — now spanning three generations of skilled craftsmen.

Every project we take on is treated as a unique challenge. We work closely with each client to understand their vision, then bring it to life with precision engineering and artistic flair. From motorized estate gates to intricate ornamental railings, we pour our expertise into every weld, every curve, every finish.

Our team combines 25+ years of hands-on fabrication experience with modern design tools to deliver results that exceed expectations — on time and on budget. As a licensed California contractor (CA Lic #1119503), you can trust that every job meets the highest standards of safety and quality.
