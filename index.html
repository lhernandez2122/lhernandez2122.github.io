FAQ

Questions Before You Book?

The stuff homeowners ask us every week — straight answers, no runaround.

How much does a custom driveway gate cost in Ventura County?

Most of our motorized driveway gates fall between $8,000 and $35,000 installed. Price drivers: width, material (wrought iron vs steel vs aluminum), ornamental detail, motor system, and site conditions. Every quote is line-itemed so you see exactly what every dollar pays for. Financing is available.

How long does a project take from first call to installation?

Free consultation within 3–5 days • Design and quote within 48 hours • Permits (if required) 2–6 weeks • Shop fabrication 3–6 weeks • Install 1–5 days. Most full projects wrap 6–10 weeks from the day you sign off on the design. Rush schedules available.

Do you handle permits and HOA approvals?

Yes. We handle permits with the City of Ventura, Oxnard, Thousand Oaks, Camarillo, Simi Valley, unincorporated Ventura County, and your HOA. Renderings, submittals, approvals — all included in the quote. You don't talk to a single planning counter.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Legendary Metal Works Inc. is a California-licensed contractor (Lic #1119503), fully insured with general liability and workers' comp. We'll email you our certificates of insurance before any contract is signed — just ask.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with flexible home-improvement lenders offering approval decisions in about 60 seconds. Plans run from 12 to 144 months. A $20,000 gate can become around $299/month — ask us for your specific options during your consultation.

What areas do you serve?

All of Ventura County, the Conejo Valley, and greater LA County: Ventura, Oxnard, Camarillo, Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Newbury Park, Simi Valley, Moorpark, Ojai, Santa Paula, Fillmore, Agoura Hills, Calabasas, and Malibu. For larger projects outside this radius, we'll travel — call to discuss.

How do you handle coastal corrosion for beachfront homes?

Homes near the ocean (Oxnard Shores, Ventura beach, Malibu) get our coastal package: 316 marine-grade stainless hardware, hot-dipped galvanizing, zinc-rich primer, marine powder coat, and stainless fasteners throughout. Adds 10–20% to the cost, doubles the lifespan.
