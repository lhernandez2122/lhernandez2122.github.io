FAQ

Consultation Questions

What happens during the consultation?

We visit your property, take measurements, discuss your design preferences and budget, and provide a detailed quote — usually within 48 hours.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, 100% free with no obligation. We believe in earning your business through our expertise and transparency.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project complexity. Simple railings may take 1-2 weeks, while custom gates can take 3-6 weeks from design approval to installation.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make your dream metalwork accessible. Ask about our financing options during your consultation.
